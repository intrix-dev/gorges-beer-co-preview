The spaces

From open-air hilltop to first-floor games

Six ways to gather at our flagship in Cascade Locks — pick one, or take the whole building.

Take the 360° tour ↗

The Hilltop

A one-acre open-air event space above the river — ceremonies & celebrations under an 1,800 sq ft tent with the Columbia and the Bridge of the Gods as your backdrop. Yard games and Adirondack chairs, long tables under the sky, its own bar and four restrooms, and seating for up to 250 guests. Waterfalls and trailheads are minutes away.

Open air · 1 acre · up to 250

3rd Floor & Rooftops

A large indoor space with its own bar, flanked by two rooftop patios with sweeping river views. Our biggest room — receptions, company parties, rehearsal dinners.

100–150 guests

1st Floor Bar & Dining

Bar, dining room, shuffleboard, cornhole, and more — the easy-going option for casual private events and big group dinners.

Up to 60 guests

Also available — the Mezzanine, a tucked-away second-floor lounge for up to 30 (bridal showers, rehearsal dinners, small parties).
