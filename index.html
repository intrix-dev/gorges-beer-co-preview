Cascade Locks · Portland, Oregon

Fresh brews. Fresh views.

A three-story brewpub over the Columbia River — rooftop patios, scratch kitchen, 16 taps, and the Gorge in every direction. Thirty minutes from Portland, a world away from it.

A place to take a break
“Not your typical brewery. We created Gorges to be spacious, inviting, and welcoming to all — take a hike, find a waterfall, then join us for a great meal.” Travis Preece, Owner

Cascade Locks is the gateway to dozens of waterfalls and breathtaking hikes. Gorges is where the day winds down: dinner and a pint on the roof, dogs and kids welcome, river traffic drifting past below.
